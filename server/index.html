高鐵以及鄉島綫獨立軌道工程-High-speed and Heung line independent track engineering

中文版本-Chinese

由於鄉島綫與高速鐵路共用部分軌道，拉低了鄉島綫與高速鐵路的班次，所以管理員一致決定重建軌道。


由於工程量巨大，有鑒於此，我們一致決定暫時停運鄉島綫與高速鐵路。

英文版本-English

Since the Heung Line and High-speed share part of the track, the frequency of the Heung Line and High-speed has been reduced, so the administrator unanimously decided to rebuild the track.

Due to the huge amount of work, in view of this, we unanimously decided to temporarily suspend the operation of Heung Line and High-speed.
